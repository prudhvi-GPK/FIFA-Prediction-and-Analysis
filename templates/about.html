About

Welcome to Goal Prediction, where I delve into the exciting world of football data to predict goal outcomes with accuracy. This project combines cutting-edge technologies such as Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA), Machine Learning, and Web Development to provide insightful analyses and predictions.

The Goal is to extract meaningful insights from football event data, explore trends and patterns, and develop predictive models to enhance understanding and enjoyment of the beautiful game.
